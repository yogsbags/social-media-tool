# Generic Brand Reference Guide

## Brand Positioning
- Use this app for broad social, campaign, and content generation across industries.
- Focus on clarity, credibility, differentiation, and customer relevance.
- Avoid legacy slogans, vague corporate filler, and overly niche financial framing unless explicitly requested.

## Common Use Cases
- Product launches and feature announcements
- Customer education and onboarding
- Thought leadership and market commentary
- Lead generation and conversion campaigns
- Case studies, testimonials, and success stories
- Event promotions and community updates

## Audience Segments
- Internal teams and leadership
- Prospects and lead-generation audiences
- Existing customers and power users
- Professionals and practitioners
- Executive and decision-maker audiences

## Message Patterns
- Explain what changed and why it matters.
- Highlight one clear benefit or insight.
- Use practical, concrete language.
- End with one clear call to action.
